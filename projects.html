Current Networking Projects

I’m tackling a variety of network engineering projects to expand my skills and home lab capabilities:

  • RetroPie Gaming Server: Building a RetroPie console on a Raspberry Pi 4B to play older and lost video games.
  • Wired Home Office Upgrade: Wiring my home office to move gaming consoles and workstations off Wi-Fi and onto a stable, gigabit Ethernet network.
  • Locally Hosted LLM: Deploying a small large language model in my lab environment for on-prem inference and privacy.
  • AI-Powered Security Cameras: Designing an enhanced home security system using Raspberry Pi boards and the official AI Camera Module.
